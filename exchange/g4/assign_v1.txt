The Hungarian algorithm only works on weighted bipartite graphs. Check this out for complete graphs: https://brilliant.org/wiki/blossom-algorithm/

Also this: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blossom_algorithm

HSV (hue, saturation, value) or HSL sorting could be a bit better. Check this discussion: https://stackoverflow.com/questions/6208980/sorting-a-list-of-rgb-triplets-into-a-spectrum

We can use hilbert curves to reduce. But I'm not sure exactly how that might benefit us.
